# Unit Economics

Unit economics helps answer: how much does one meaningful unit of value cost to deliver?

## Candidate units
- one approved change
- one released workflow
- one validated artifact
- one customer-facing action
- one supportable decision

## Why it matters
This makes invisible scale effects visible.

For AI-agent systems, the relevant unit is often not a raw request count, but a workflow completion or approved artifact. That makes it easier to compare competing designs and control hidden expansion in loops, reviews, and retries.
