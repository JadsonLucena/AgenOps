# Flow Model

## Goal
Optimize for flow efficiency while preserving governance integrity.

## Primary flow
1. Stream-aligned team identifies a need.
2. Team consumes the platform contract or requests support.
3. Enabling team coaches if adoption friction exists.
4. Platform team evolves the kit only when the need is reusable.
5. Evidence bundle is produced for any approval gate.
6. Approved change is archived as a durable artifact.

## Anti-patterns
- platform team acting as a ticket queue
- stream teams bypassing the kit
- enabling team becoming a shadow platform
- local exceptions becoming de facto standards

## Throughput rule
Do not centralize every decision.

Centralize only:
- contract versioning
- validation logic
- policy definitions
- canonical state ownership
- cross-cutting security gates
