# Judge Rubric

## Dimensions (score 0–5 each)

- clarity
- completeness
- consistency
- correctness
- security_awareness
- maintainability
- performance_awareness
- cost_awareness

## Final score
sum(dimensions) * 2 → normalized to 0–100

## Thresholds
- 85–100: strong
- 70–84: acceptable
- 50–69: weak
- <50: reject

## Rules
- any score <2 in critical dimensions → automatic revise
