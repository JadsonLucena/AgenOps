# Exploration Protocol

This protocol defines when to use SoT and ToT.

## Use SoT when
- the goal is to produce a complex artifact
- the artifact needs structure before detail
- the artifact must be reviewable in parts

## Use ToT when
- the goal is to make a hard decision
- multiple alternatives are credible
- trade-offs need explicit comparison

## Do not use either when
- the task is simple
- the answer is deterministic
- validation already determines the outcome

## Rule
SoT and ToT are complementary, not interchangeable.
