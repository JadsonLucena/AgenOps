# Data Handling Policy

## Scope

Prevent unnecessary retention or disclosure of secrets and confidential data.

## Requirements

- Do not store secrets in documents or prompts.
- Treat personal or confidential data as minimized, temporary, and purpose-specific.
- Avoid copying large raw logs into long-term artifacts.
- Redact sensitive values in examples and reports.

## Exceptions

- Deviations require explicit rationale and approval when they affect shared behavior, security, or compatibility.

## Evidence

- review notes
- validation output
- linked change record
