Canonical decision framework lives in `governance/decision/`.

# Decision Rationale Policy

## Scope

Prefer concise decision summaries over raw internal reasoning artifacts.

## Requirements

Use concise decision summaries instead of raw chain-of-thought.
- decision
- context
- alternatives
- trade-offs
- selected option
- consequences
- follow-up actions
- verbose internal reasoning
- uncurated brainstorm notes
- repeated re-explanations of the same choice

## Exceptions

- Deviations require explicit rationale and approval when they affect shared behavior, security, or compatibility.

## Evidence

- review notes
- validation output
- linked change record
